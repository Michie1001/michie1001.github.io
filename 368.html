368

368 is where creators create in NYC. We are new but our ambitions are to be a hub for aspiring and established creators. Also we have a dog named Rolo

Our Story

Innovators. Creators.

Located at 368 Broadway, 368 is housed in a three-story building with no upstairs or downstairs neighbors to complain about the racket that is sure to be made. Casey envisions all types of creators inhabiting 368 Broadway. Whether it’s foreign vloggers visiting the States who need a room to get some editing done, or local podcasters looking to broadcast from the heart of New York, the guest list is inclusive of anyone who creates.
It’s a bunch of giant rooms, wood floors, and exposed piping. Beautiful, yes. Seeing as this is Casey Neistat we’re talking about, the project wouldn’t be complete without producing some great content.
